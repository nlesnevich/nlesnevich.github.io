Research Interests

I am broadly interested in Algebraic and Enumerative Combinatorics, in particular with connections to Geometry and Representation Theory.

I currently work on representations of Weyl groups on splines, immanants of Jacobi-Trudi matrices, and questions related to the cohomology of Hessenberg- and other GKM-varieties.
